Project Description

On October 18th 2020, my partner Martin Yushko and I competed in a hackathon put on by DubHacks. Despite our lack of experience, we glued ourselves to our desks, stayed up all night, and tried our very hardest to develop something great. What resulted was something we called "inHABITation" - an app that gamifies your life and motivates you to crush your goals through competing with your friends.

Following the competition, Dubhacks personally emailed us and explained that our project was just shy of making the top 3. They further went on to complement the idea and encouraged us to develop the app further. Thrilled by this feedback, I took it upon myself to assemble a team to get the job done. The app is currently in production, and will be released soon.

If you are interested, you can download the excel spreadsheet that is ultimately the motivation behind this project. I developed this in highschool without any excel experience, so please excuse its poor design: XP Chart
